    Meet Christiana Lamba

    ABOUT CHRISTIANA

    Christiana is a graduate of Law. She also holds a certificate in presentation from the Federal Radio Corporation of Nigeria (FRCN), and had training from Speedwriting International Lagos. She has been freelance transcribing and minute-reporting for the last 6 years. She speaks four languages including French and has held business positions in various public and private companies in Nigeria and West Africa.

    She has worked within the direct sales industry in Nigeria as administrative assistant and at Infinite Insight, a market research firm in Lagos as a qualitative researcher where she developed her managerial skills.

    An entrepreneur, she founded MAKARIOS TOUCH in 2011 to bridge the gap for SMEs needing scribing, transcribing & accurate editing services.

    A member of The Nigerian Red Cross Society, She enjoys travelling, learning new languages, new recipes and music.
