TRANSCRIPTION

Accurate, Confidential, Transparent, Simple, Consistent

Before 1970, transcription was a difficult job as secretaries had to write down the speech as they heard it using advanced skills like shorthand. But with the introduction of portable tape recorders, the work became much easier and more possibilities emerged.

A transcription service is one which converts speech (recorded) into electronic text document. Providing a text alternative for your audio files is required by law in some cases; in others, it's just the smart thing to do. It means that you can reach a wider audience and share information quickly and easily with people across the world and in a manner which fits their busy schedule. Podcasts, videos, and other audio files must include a transcript.
Our transcription is the most cost-effective option to meet your needs, with both basic services and premium-level features. Special discounts are also available for large volume projects.

Benefits of
Transcription

  • In many cases transcripts are required by law (in some countries) to provide access to information for people who are deaf or hard of hearing. They too can also benefit from your materials.
  • Studies have shown that people absorb text based matter more accurately when compared to audios.
  • Transcripts can be converted into a manuscript or book and published as an E book.
  • People who have difficulty processing auditory information, for example, because of cognitive disability.
  • People who are not proficient in the language who find it easier to read than listen.
  • People who won't spend the time to listen to the audio or watch the video, but will skim a transcript.
  • People who cannot play the audio because they are in a noisy environment and they can't hear it.
  • People who cannot play the audio because they are in a quiet environment and they don't want to disturb others.
  • More traffic to your information. Your material can be published on your website or on the internet, meaning that they are searchable and easy to share.
  • SEO - search engine optimization. Search engines can index the transcript, not the audio or video. It's easier for people to link to a transcript than some audio or video files.
  • Sermons can be uploaded to website.
  • Speeches can be saved in archives/ libraries.
  • Trainings and teachings can be sent via emails to participants.
