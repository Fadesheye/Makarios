    Benefits of Transcription

    Why Transcribe?

    In many cases transcripts are required by law (in some countries) to provide access to information for people who are deaf or hard of hearing. They too can also benefit from your materials.

    People who have difficulty processing auditory information, for example, because of cognitive disability.

    SEO - search engine optimization. Search engines can index the transcript, not the audio or video. It's easier for people to link to a transcript than some audio or video files.

    More traffic to your information. Your material can be published on your website or on the internet, meaning that they are searchable and easy to share.

    People who won't spend the time to listen to the audio or watch the video, but will skim a transcript.

    Sermons can be uploaded to website.
